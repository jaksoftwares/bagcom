BAGCOM Marketplace Platform
Brand Identity & Design System Documentation
1. Introduction
1.1 Purpose of This Document

This document defines the official branding, visual identity, typography system, design language, and brand guidelines for the Bagcom marketplace platform.

The purpose of the branding system is to ensure:

Consistency across all platforms
Strong visual identity
User trust
Professional presentation
Scalable UI/UX standards
Clear marketplace recognition
Cohesive product experience

This branding guide serves as the official reference for:

Designers
Frontend developers
Marketing teams
Product managers
Social media teams
Print and media production
UI/UX implementation
2. Brand Overview
Brand Name

Bagcom

Brand Positioning

Bagcom is positioned as:

A trusted, modern, community-driven marketplace for secure second-hand commerce.

The platform combines:

Simplicity
Trust
Community
Technology
Safety
Accessibility
Local commerce
3. Brand Mission
Mission Statement

To provide a secure, accessible, and community-powered digital marketplace that enables people to buy and sell second-hand goods safely using trusted escrow-based payments.

4. Brand Vision
Vision Statement

To become Africa’s leading trusted second-hand digital marketplace built around secure local commerce and community-driven trade.
